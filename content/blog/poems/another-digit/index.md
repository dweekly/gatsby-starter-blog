---
title: Another Digit
date: 1996-01-01
---

Pushing numbers  
up one  
down one.

computing  
thinking

pondering

the numbers flew by

the pencil SCRATCHes against the paper  
in a flurry

one would think  
the graphite should burn

still writing

new formulae

keep going

keep going

push a number out of the way,  
once helpful, now useless,  
meaningless  
into the trash  
it goes.

It helped

  
once
